Business Agnostic Trust Solutions ()
Definition: Business Agnostic Trust Solutions (ABB) is a grouping that covers a range of solutions designed to establish and enhance trust in digital business environments. These solutions are agnostic, meaning they can be applied across various business sectors and are not limited to a specific industry or type of business. The elements within this grouping are geared towards ensuring compliance with digital trust regulations, enhancing security, and promoting transparency in digital transactions. Source: EIRA Team Research Additional information: The Business Agnostic Trust Solutions grouping includes elements such as the Digital Europe eSignature DSS open-source library. This is a software library for digital signature creation, validation, and extension. It is designed to help digital solutions achieve compliance with the eIDAS Regulation, which sets out the rules for electronic identification and trust services for electronic transactions in the European Single Market. The DSS library is a key tool for businesses looking to enhance the security and trustworthiness of their digital transactions, regardless of their specific industry or sector. Example: An example of a Business Agnostic Trust Solution is the use of the Digital Europe eSignature DSS open-source library by an e-commerce company. The company can use the DSS library to create and validate digital signatures for online transactions, ensuring compliance with eIDAS Regulation and enhancing the security and trustworthiness of their business operations.
