Health Dataset Catalogue Solutions ()
Definition: Health Dataset Catalogue Solutions ABB is a grouping that covers a range of data catalogues specifically tailored for the health sector. These catalogues contain comprehensive and detailed information about various aspects of health, including professional qualifications, health services, patient data, and more. The purpose of these elements is to provide a structured and organized source of health-related data that can be easily accessed and utilized for various purposes. Source: EIRA Team Research Additional information: The Health Dataset Catalogue Solutions ABB includes elements like the 'European health professional qualification catalogue ISCO-08', which is an European data catalogue containing the list of all the health professional habilitations. These catalogues serve as a valuable resource for health professionals, researchers, policymakers, and others who need access to detailed, accurate, and up-to-date health data. They can be used for a variety of purposes, including research, policy development, health service planning, and more. Example: European health professional qualification catalogue ISCO-08 An example of an element in the Health Dataset Catalogue Solutions ABB is the 'European health professional qualification catalogue ISCO-08'. This is a data catalogue that contains a comprehensive list of all the health professional qualifications in Europe. It can be used by health professionals to verify qualifications, by researchers to study trends in health professional qualifications, and by policymakers to develop policies related to health professional qualifications.
