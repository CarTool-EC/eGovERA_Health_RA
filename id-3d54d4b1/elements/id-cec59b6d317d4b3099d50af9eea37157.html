Health eDispensation Enablers ()
Definition: Health eDispensation Enablers ABB is a Grouping that covers a range of application services designed to facilitate the electronic dispensation of medications. These services include Pharmacy Information System (PIS) and Medicines/Medications System (MS), both of which play crucial roles in the management and dispensation of medications in a healthcare setting. Source: EIRA Team Research Additional information: The Health eDispensation Enablers ABB is a collection of application services that are integral to the digital transformation of healthcare, particularly in the area of medication dispensation. The Pharmacy Information System (PIS) is an application service that manages the storage, dispensation, and record-keeping of medications within a pharmacy. The Medicines/Medications System (MS) is another application service that manages the prescription, administration, and tracking of medications within a healthcare setting. These systems work together to ensure the safe, efficient, and accurate dispensation of medications, thereby improving patient safety and healthcare outcomes. Example: For instance, a Pharmacy Information System (PIS) can be used in a hospital pharmacy to manage the inventory of medications, track their usage, and generate reports for regulatory compliance. Similarly, a Medicines/Medications System (MS) can be used by healthcare providers to prescribe medications, track their administration, and monitor patient responses. These systems can also be integrated with other healthcare IT systems to provide a comprehensive view of a patient's medication history, thereby supporting clinical decision-making and patient care.
