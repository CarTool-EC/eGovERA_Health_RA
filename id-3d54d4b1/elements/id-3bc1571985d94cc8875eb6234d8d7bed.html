Health Base Registry Solutions ()
Definition: Health Base Registry Solutions ABB is a Grouping that covers a range of solutions designed to enhance the efficiency, reliability, and timeliness of health-related diagnostic services. The solutions are specifically targeted towards the management and control of diseases, with a particular focus on tuberculosis (TB). Source: EIRA Team Research Additional information: The Health Base Registry Solutions ABB includes elements like the European Reference Laboratory Network for TB (ERLTB-Net). Launched by the ECDC in January 2010, the ERLTB-Net is a network of officially nominated laboratories across EU/EEA countries with reference functions for diagnostics and/or molecular typing of TB. The network supports countries in ensuring the provision of reliable and timely diagnostic services and aids the ECDC in its alert and response functions relating to TB outbreaks. Example: An example of the application of the Health Base Registry Solutions ABB is the European Reference Laboratory Network for TB (ERLTB-Net). This network, as part of the Health Base Registry Solutions, provides a platform for EU/EEA countries to ensure the provision of reliable and timely diagnostic services for TB. It also supports the ECDC in alerting and responding to TB outbreaks.
