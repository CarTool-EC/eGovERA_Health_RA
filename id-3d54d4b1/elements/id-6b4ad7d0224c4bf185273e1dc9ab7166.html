Health Hospital Enterprise Resource Management Enablers ()
Definition: Health Hospital Enterprise Resource Management Enablers ABB is a Grouping that covers a range of elements designed to facilitate the management of resources within a healthcare setting. These elements, which include the Health Professionals Skills System (HPSS) and the Hospital Blood Bank System, are application services that support the efficient operation of a hospital or healthcare enterprise. Source: EIRA Team Research Additional information: The Health Hospital Enterprise Resource Management Enablers ABB encompasses two main elements: the Health Professionals Skills System (HPSS) and the Hospital Blood Bank System. The HPSS is an application service that helps manage and optimize the skills of healthcare professionals, ensuring that the right professionals are assigned to the right tasks. The Hospital Blood Bank System, on the other hand, is an application service that manages the hospital's blood bank, tracking blood donations, storage, and usage to ensure efficient and safe blood supply management. Both of these systems are crucial for the smooth operation of a healthcare enterprise, enabling efficient resource management and improved patient care. Example: For instance, the Health Professionals Skills System (HPSS) could be used in a large hospital to manage the scheduling of nurses and doctors, ensuring that each department has the necessary skills coverage at all times. The Hospital Blood Bank System could be used in the same hospital to manage the inventory of blood and blood products, tracking their usage and ensuring that there is always an adequate supply available for patient needs.
