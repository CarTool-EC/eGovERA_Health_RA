TVA-Health Technical Agreements ()
NARRATIVE: The TVA-Health Technical Agreements view extends Technical view - application and provides a comprehensive framework for leveraging various types of contracts and groupings to ensure technical interoperability and service quality in the health sector. The view includes three types of Contract ABBs: Technical Agreement, Technical Interoperability Agreement, and Service Level Agreement. The Technical Agreement formalizes a framework for strategic technological collaboration, while the Technical Interoperability Agreement ensures organizations operate under different technical frameworks. The Service Level Agreement outlines the level of service provided and the metrics used to measure service quality. The view also includes several Grouping ABBs, each containing multiple Contract SBBs. These groupings represent different areas of technical agreement solutions in the health sector, including Health Technical Interoperability Agreement Solutions, Health Medical Technical Agreement Solutions, Health Data Exchange Technical Agreement Solutions, Health Hospital Enterprise Resource Management Technical Interoperability Agreement Solutions, Health eDispensation Data Exchange Technical Interoperability Agreement Solutions, Health Patient Summary Technical Interoperability Agreement Solutions, Health Medical Imaging Workflow Data Exchange Technical Interoperability Agreement Solutions, and Health Laboratory Technical Interoperability Agreement Solutions. Each Grouping ABB contains multiple Contract SBBs that represent specific technical agreements within the grouping's area. For example, the Health Technical Interoperability Agreement Solutions grouping includes the eHealth DSI technical framework (cross-border) Contract SBB. The Health Medical Technical Agreement Solutions grouping includes Contract SBBs such as UMLS - Unified Medical Language System, C-CDA -Clinical documents, Health informatics -- Detailed clinical models, characteristics and processes (ISO/TS 13972), and MeSH - Medical Subject Headings. Each Contract SBB within a Grouping ABB represents a specific technical agreement that contributes to the overall interoperability and service quality in the grouping's area. For example, the UMLS - Unified Medical Language System Contract SBB in the Health Medical Technical Agreement Solutions grouping represents a specific agreement on the use of the Unified Medical Language System in health medical technology. In summary, the TVA-Health Technical Agreements view provides a comprehensive framework for leveraging various types of contracts and groupings to ensure technical interoperability and service quality in the health sector. The view's structure and elements facilitate the identification of common interests, policy dialogue, and the necessary tools for strategic technological collaboration.
