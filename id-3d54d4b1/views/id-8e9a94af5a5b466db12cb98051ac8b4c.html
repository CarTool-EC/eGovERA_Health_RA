Narrative: The "Technical view - Application Orchestration" viewpoint extends technical view application and provides a comprehensive framework for coordinating and automating complex workflows and processes within digital applications. This view associates the Application Orchestration with Application Orchestration Business Services from the application layer. Within this view, Application Orchestration refers to the management and coordination of various tasks, services, and components within digital applications. It involves the automation of workflows, the sequencing of activities, and the integration of disparate systems to achieve efficient and seamless execution of processes. Application Orchestration Business Services leverage the Application Orchestration to enable organizations to streamline their operations, automate repetitive tasks, and optimize the overall performance of their digital applications. These services provide a centralized mechanism for defining, managing, and monitoring complex workflows and processes within the digital ecosystem. By associating the Application Orchestration with Application Orchestration Business Services, the "Technical view - Application Orchestration" empowers organizations to achieve efficient and coordinated execution of tasks, ensuring seamless integration and optimized performance within their digital applications. The use of Application Orchestration enables organizations to automate business processes, reduce manual intervention, and improve overall operational efficiency. It provides the ability to define and manage complex workflows, ensure task dependencies and sequencing, and handle exceptions or error conditions within digital applications.
