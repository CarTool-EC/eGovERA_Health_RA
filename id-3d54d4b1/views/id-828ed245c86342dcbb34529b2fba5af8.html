TVA-Digital Workplace Enablers [Motivation] ()
Narrative: The "Technical view - Application Digital Workplace" viewpoint extends technical view application and provides a comprehensive framework for facilitating digital collaboration, communication, and productivity within organizations. This view associates the Application Digital Workplace with Digital Workplace Business Services from the application layer. Within this view, the Application Digital Workplace represents the digital platform or suite of applications that enable employees to perform their work-related tasks, collaborate with colleagues, access resources, and engage in communication within a digital environment. It encompasses tools such as messaging apps, document sharing platforms, task management systems, and virtual meeting applications. Digital Workplace Business Services leverage the Application Digital Workplace to provide employees with a centralized and integrated digital work environment. These services enable seamless collaboration, efficient communication, and easy access to business resources, fostering productivity and teamwork within organizations. By associating the Application Digital Workplace with Digital Workplace Business Services, the "Technical view - Application Digital Workplace" promotes a cohesive and user-friendly digital work environment, enabling employees to work efficiently and effectively. The use of the Application Digital Workplace allows employees to access relevant information, collaborate on projects, and communicate with colleagues regardless of their physical location. It provides a unified platform where employees can find and access the tools and resources they need to perform their work tasks.
