TVA-Health Patient Summary Enablers [Motivation] ()
NARRATIVE: The TVA-Health Patient Summary Enablers [Motivation] view extends Technical view - application and provides a comprehensive framework for leveraging various application services, business services, and contracts to enable effective health patient summary management. The view is divided into four main sections: Health Patient Summary Enablers, eGovERA Health Digital Public Services Catalogue, Business Agnostic Digital Public Services Catalogue, and Health Patient Summary Technical Interoperability Agreement Solutions. The Health Patient Summary Enablers section includes a range of application services such as the Admission, Discharge and Transfer System (ADTS), Identification and Registration System (IRS), Engagement and Self-management System (ESMS), Electronic Health Records System (EHRS), Patient summary system, Patient Management Information System, Clinical Information System (CIS), Social Service and Patients' Relations system (SSPRS), Chronic Care Management System (CCMS), Medical Devices System (MDS), Implants Registry System (IRS), Diagnosis system, Health remote sensing and continuous monitoring, and Telemetry Monitoring System (TMS). These services are grouped together as they provide the necessary functionalities for managing health patient summaries. The eGovERA Health Digital Public Services Catalogue section includes business services such as Patient summary, Health remote sensing and continuous monitoring, Tele-diagnosis, Medical devices/equipments, and Medical equipment resource allocation. These services are part of a digital public services catalogue specifically designed for the health sector. The Business Agnostic Digital Public Services Catalogue section includes a Workflow Management business service, which is a digital public service that is not specific to any business sector. Another eGovERA Health Digital Public Services Catalogue section includes business services such as Patient digital twin, Electronic Healthcare Records, and Healthcare enterprise resource management. These services are also part of the digital public services catalogue specifically designed for the health sector. The Health Patient Summary Technical Interoperability Agreement Solutions section includes a range of contracts such as IHE PDQ: Patient Demographics Query, IHE PIX: Patient Identifier Cross-Referencing, PHR-S FM - Personal Health Record System Functional Model, IHE XCPD: Cross-Community Patient Discovery, IHE XD-MS: Cross-Enterprise Sharing of Medical Summaries Integration Profile, IHE BPPC: Basic Patient Privacy Consents, IHE PAM: Patient Administration Management, IHE PIR: Patient Information Reconciliation, CEN/TS 17288 - The International Patient Summary - Guideline for European Implementation, Clinical Data Interchange Standards Consortium - CDISC, and IHE XPHR: Exchange of Personal Health Record Content. These contracts represent technical interoperability agreement solutions for health patient summary management. The relations between these elements are not explicitly defined in the JSON, but it can be inferred that the application services in the Health Patient Summary Enablers section are used to enable the business services in the eGovERA Health Digital Public Services Catalogue and Business Agnostic Digital Public Services Catalogue sections. The contracts in the Health Patient Summary Technical Interoperability Agreement Solutions section are likely used to ensure interoperability between the different application services and business services. The motivation behind this view is to provide a comprehensive framework for managing health patient summaries effectively and efficiently, leveraging digital technologies and interoperability standards.
