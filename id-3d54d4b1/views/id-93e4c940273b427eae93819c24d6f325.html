TVA-Artificial Intelligence Enablers [Motivation] ()
The "Technical view - Application Artificial Intelligence" viewpoint extends technical view application and provides a comprehensive framework for leveraging artificial intelligence capabilities within digital applications. This view associates the Application Artificial Intelligence with Artificial Intelligence Services from the application layer. Within this view, Application Artificial Intelligence refers to the integration of artificial intelligence techniques and algorithms into digital applications to enhance their capabilities, automate tasks, and enable intelligent decision-making. It encompasses various technologies such as machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision. Digital applications leverage Application Artificial Intelligence to deliver enhanced functionalities, improve user experiences, and enable intelligent automation. By utilizing artificial intelligence services, organizations can extract insights, recognize patterns, and make predictions from large volumes of data, enabling personalized experiences and intelligent recommendations within the digital ecosystem. By associating Application Artificial Intelligence with Artificial Intelligence Services, the "Technical view - Application Artificial Intelligence" empowers organizations to harness the power of artificial intelligence to drive innovation and efficiency within their digital applications. The use of Application Artificial Intelligence allows organizations to automate repetitive tasks, gain deeper insights from data, and deliver personalized experiences to users. It enables applications to adapt and learn from user interactions, improving over time and providing intelligent responses to user queries or requests.
