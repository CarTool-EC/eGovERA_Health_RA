TVA-Health Hospital Enterprise Resource Management Enablers [Motivation] ()
NARRATIVE: The TVA-Health Hospital Enterprise Resource Management Enablers [Motivation] view extends Technical view - application and provides a comprehensive framework for leveraging various elements to improve the efficiency and effectiveness of healthcare services. The view includes a Grouping ABB named "Health Hospital Enterprise Resource Management Enablers" which encompasses two Application Service ABBs. The first is the "Health Professionals Skills System (HPSS)" which is an application service designed to manage and enhance the skills of health professionals. The second is the "Hospital Blood Bank System", another application service that manages the hospital's blood bank resources. Another key component of this view is the "eGovERA Health Digital Public Services Catalogue", a Diagram Model-group ABB that includes a Business Service ABB named "Healthcare enterprise resource management". This service enables the management of resources within the healthcare system. The view also includes a "Business Agnostic Digital Public Services Catalogue", another Diagram Model-group ABB. This catalogue includes two Business Service ABBs: "Knowledge Management", a digital public service focused on managing and disseminating knowledge, and "Data Exchange", a service that facilitates the exchange of data. Finally, the view includes a Grouping ABB named "Health Hospital Enterprise Resource Management Technical Interoperability Agreement Solutions". This grouping includes a Contract SBB named "IHE SVS: Sharing Value Sets", which outlines the terms for sharing value sets. The motivation behind these elements and their relations is to create a comprehensive and integrated system for managing resources in a healthcare setting. This includes enhancing the skills of health professionals, managing blood bank resources, enabling resource management within the healthcare system, managing and disseminating knowledge, facilitating data exchange, and sharing value sets. This system aims to improve the efficiency and effectiveness of healthcare services, ultimately leading to better patient outcomes.
