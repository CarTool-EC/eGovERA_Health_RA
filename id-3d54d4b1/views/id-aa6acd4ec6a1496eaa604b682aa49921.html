TVA-Knowledge Discovery Enablers [Motivation] ()
Narrative: The "Technical view - Application Knowledge Discovery" viewpoint extends technical view application and provides a comprehensive framework for leveraging knowledge discovery capabilities within digital applications. This view associates the Application Knowledge Discovery with Knowledge Discovery Business Services from the application layer. Within this view, Application Knowledge Discovery refers to the process of extracting meaningful insights, patterns, and knowledge from large volumes of data within digital applications. It encompasses techniques such as data mining, machine learning, and natural language processing to uncover hidden patterns and derive valuable insights. Knowledge Discovery Business Services leverage Application Knowledge Discovery to enable organizations to gain actionable insights, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions. By utilizing knowledge discovery techniques, organizations can unlock valuable knowledge and extract meaningful information from their data within the digital ecosystem. By associating the Application Knowledge Discovery with Knowledge Discovery Business Services, the "Technical view - Application Knowledge Discovery" empowers organizations to leverage advanced analytical techniques and algorithms to uncover insights and enhance decision-making processes within their digital applications. The use of Application Knowledge Discovery allows organizations to automate the process of extracting knowledge from data, reducing manual effort and uncovering valuable insights that can drive business success. It enables organizations to discover patterns, make predictions, and gain a deeper understanding of their data, enabling informed decision-making and improving operational efficiency.
